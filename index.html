Data Description¶

Context¶

Since 2008, Airbnb has been a platform for travelers having another option of staying in local hosts home different from hotel and resort

Content¶

The original source is from the database of Airbnbs websit opened to the public. The csv file includes all needed information to find out more about hosts, geographical availability, necessary metrics

last_review:latest review

calculated_host_listings_count:amount of listing per host

Customer Churn¶

From the report of availability_365 has 1119 (11.3%) zeros, Airbnb may lose the source of host or the owners would temporarily close. Take a look at the data by areas, room types and prices.Lodgings are closed by guests checking in or hosts stopping using Airbnb could cause the unavailable status.

  1. The number of the hosts stopping using Airbnb is 327, and Customer Churn rate is 3.29%
